# Cheeseball with Pineapple Recipe
## Ingredients:
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh pineapple
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 cup chopped pecans or walnuts (optional)
## Instructions:
1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
2. Add the mayonnaise, cheddar cheese, pineapple, parsley, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.
3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
4. When ready to serve, shape the mixture into a ball. If using nuts, roll the cheeseball in them to coat.
5. Serve the cheeseball with crackers, chips, or vegetables.
